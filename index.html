Innovatief Technologies

Welcome to Innovatief Technologies, your trusted partner in innovative animal nutrition solutions powered by cutting-edge digital technologies. We are committed to transforming the dairy, poultry, and swine sectors with data-driven insights, precision nutrition, and advanced digital tools, ensuring better productivity, animal health, and sustainable farming practices.

Vision

To be the leading provider of digital animal nutrition solutions that revolutionize livestock health, performance, and profitability through data science, precision feeding, and smart farm management technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers, feed mills, and integrators with smart digital tools for precision nutrition and animal health management, while promoting sustainable animal agriculture through efficient feed formulation, real-time farm monitoring, and advanced data analytics. We strive to foster collaborative innovation by bringing together nutrition science, technology, and local expertise to build a resilient and thriving livestock industry.

Objective

☸ To integrate precision nutrition, digital monitoring, and predictive analytics for optimized livestock performance.
☸ To provide real-time insights on feed quality, animal health, and environmental impact.
☸ To address feed safety, animal welfare, and resource efficiency through digital transformation.
☸ To develop customizable technology solutions for small-scale farms, commercial dairies, poultry farms, and swine operations.
☸ To enhance knowledge sharing and skill development through digital training modules and advisory services.

About Us

At Innovatief Technologies, we are driven by a singular mission — to transform India’s animal nutrition and livestock sectors through cutting-edge digital technologies, innovative nutrition solutions, and a strong focus on skill development. Combining decades of expertise in agriculture, animal health, and nutrition innovation, our leadership team brings a unique blend of scientific knowledge, business acumen, and technological foresight to every solution we offer.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between scientific research, technology innovation, and on-ground farming realities, Innovatief Technologies is a technology-first company focused on enhancing the productivity, profitability, and sustainability of India’s dairy, poultry, and livestock sectors.

Why Choose Us

Our Expertise

At Innovatief Technologies, we bring together decades of hands-on experience across animal nutrition, animal health, Agri-research and development, feed milling systems and processes and significant understanding of digital tools usage in innovations. With leadership backgrounds in global animal nutrition companies, veterinary pharmaceuticals, agribusiness consulting, and technology development, we understand the unique challenges faced by farmers, feed mills, and the livestock industry. Our deep-rooted knowledge in both scientific research and practical farm management ensures that our solutions are not only technically advanced, but also grounded in real-world applicability.

Innovations & Technologies

We harness the power of innovations to transform animal nutrition and health management systems. From precision nutrition, advanced feeding and feed formulation technology to feed mill management solutions, we offer a complete technology solutions designed to boost productivity, efficiency, and profitability across Livestock business. By integrating knowledge platforms and right deployment of technology tools; we enable data-driven decision-making for better animal nutrition, animal health, improved feed efficiency, and sustainable farm and feed mill operations.

Know How

Our strength lies in our deep understanding of the tropical livestock ecosystem, combined with global insights from leading markets across South Asia and beyond. We understand the critical role of skill development, digital literacy, and capacity building in ensuring successful technology adoption. Through innovative training programs, digital advisory and farmer-friendly solutions, we empower stakeholders across the value chain from farmers, nutritionists, feed mill supervisors and managers, milk processors and poultry integrator. We at Innovatief technologies believes to co-create solutions customised to the specific needs of livestock farmers and Feed Millers.
